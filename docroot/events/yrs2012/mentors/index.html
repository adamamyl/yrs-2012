Young Rewired State 2012

YRS happens in centres across the UK - centre is a business that has space and wifi to take up to 10 kids.

  • We assign kids from central sign up as well as local recruiting (by centres)

  • We ensure that each centre has at least one mentor for the Monday - Thursday

  • We are trying to assign one alumnus per centre this year as well (but that might not be possible)

Mentors will:

  • help the kids get ideas in the first place (Monday)

  • understand the data

  • help with programming where necessary

  • do stand up presentations with the kids every day

  • liaise with each other and share skills remotely if necessary

  • assist with the prep for final presentation

  • be invited to mentor at TNMOC (Bletchley Park) on the Friday/Saturday when all the kids are together

FAQs:

  • Will mentors get Tee Shirts? YES

  • Will travel and subsistence be covered for mentors? Assuming we get sponsorship we are aiming for then yes - if not then there will be a hardship pot

  • Do mentors need to be available every day of the week 6-11th August? Not at all, we will be in touch and will work out which days you are free. But BE WARNED, it is very addictive and compelling and you will want to be there, especially at the end!

  • Can I chat to a mentor who has done it before? Yes, we plan to ask a few mentors from previous years to be available for queries - in the first instance please contact David Durant dave@bowsy.co.uk

  • Do I need to be CRB checked? No BUT there will be no 121 mentoring, everything will happen in a single open space (this will be the centres to sort) We are looking into the rules for the festival at the end of the week. So watch this space

Who are Rewired State?

We run hack days.
We take between 10 – 150 talented developers and give them money, time, space, caffeine, sugar and food, whilst they build cool/creative prototypes to solve your problems.
If you'd like to kickstart a new project or accelerate an existing Research & Development programme, get in touch.
